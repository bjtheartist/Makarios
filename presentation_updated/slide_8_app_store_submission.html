App Store Submission

Getting Your App to the World
Requirements
  • Apple Developer Account
    $99/year membership fee
  • Google Play Console
    $25 one-time registration fee
  • Store Assets
    Icons, screenshots (all sizes)
  • Legal Documents
    Privacy Policy & Terms of Service
Deployment Options
Web App (PWA)
Immediate

Launch immediately without store review. Users can add to home screen. No fees, instant updates, but less discoverability.

Native App
Recommended

Full presence on App Store and Google Play. Higher trust, better discoverability, and access to native features. Requires 1-2 week review.
