Content Management

How to Add Content Today

File Location

All content is centralized in /Makarios/constants.ts for easy access and modification.

Data Structure

Content is organized into clear arrays (e.g., PRAYERS, LESSONS) making it intuitive to navigate.

Editing Process

Simply modify the text within the objects to update prayers, questions, or video details instantly.

Future Evolution

This static file will be replaced by a user-friendly Headless CMS in the next development phase.
