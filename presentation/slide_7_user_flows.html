User Flows

How Users Navigate the App

Flow 01

Guest User

Users can explore the full app immediately without signing up, significantly lowering the barrier to entry and building trust.

Flow 02

Email Registration

New users sign up, encounter the subscription paywall, select a plan, and gain full access to premium content.

Flow 03

Returning User

Existing users log in securely. The system checks their subscription status and directs them to the app or the renewal page.
