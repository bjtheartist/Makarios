What is Makarios?

A premium Christian discipleship app designed for spiritual growth

Name Origin
"Makarios" is Greek for "blessed" or "happy," reflecting the mission to guide users toward a blessed life through faith.
Mission
To help users deepen their faith through structured content, guided prayers, biblical education, and community testimonies.
Target Audience
Christians seeking spiritual growth, prayer guidance, and biblical education.
Monetization
Subscription-based at $12.99/month or $97/year, with a 7-day free trial.
