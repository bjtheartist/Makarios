# Makarios Content Management Guide

Welcome, founder! This guide will walk you through how to manage all the content in your app using the Sanity Content Studio. This is your control center for everything your users will see.

## 1. Accessing Your Content Studio

Your Content Studio is where you can add, edit, and delete content. It is a user-friendly interface that requires no coding.

**How to Access:**

1.  **Go to your deployed Studio URL:**
    [https://makarios.sanity.studio](https://makarios.sanity.studio) (or your custom URL)

2.  **Log in** with your Google, GitHub, or email account.

## 2. Managing Your Content

Once you log in, you will see a list of your content types on the left-hand side. Here’s a breakdown of each one and how to manage them:

### **Prayers**

-   **To Add a New Prayer:**
    1.  Click on **"Prayer"** in the left menu.
    2.  Click the **"Create new"** button (a plus icon).
    3.  Fill in the fields:
        -   **Title:** The name of the prayer.
        -   **Category:** Choose from the dropdown (e.g., Morning, Evening).
        -   **Prayer Content:** The full text of the prayer.
        -   **Scripture Reference:** The Bible verse that inspired it.
        -   **Premium:** Check this if it’s for subscribers only.
    4.  Click **"Publish"** to make it live.

### **Bible Lessons**

-   **To Add a New Lesson:**
    1.  Click on **"Bible Lesson"**.
    2.  Click **"Create new"**.
    3.  Fill in the fields:
        -   **Title:** The lesson title.
        -   **Description:** A short summary.
        -   **Lesson Content:** Use the rich text editor to write the lesson.
        -   **Thumbnail:** Upload a cover image.
        -   **Premium:** Check if it’s a premium lesson.
    4.  Click **"Publish"**.

### **Video Courses & Videos**

-   **To Add a New Video:**
    1.  First, make sure you have a **"Video Course"** created.
    2.  Click on **"Video"** and **"Create new"**.
    3.  Fill in the fields:
        -   **Title:** The video title.
        -   **Video URL:** Paste the YouTube or Vimeo link.
        -   **Course:** Select the course it belongs to.
        -   **Premium:** Check if it’s a premium video.
    4.  Click **"Publish"**.

### **Bible Trivia**

-   **To Add a New Trivia Question:**
    1.  First, ensure the **"Trivia Book"** exists (e.g., Genesis, Matthew).
    2.  Click on **"Trivia Question"** and **"Create new"**.
    3.  Fill in the fields:
        -   **Question:** The trivia question.
        -   **Book:** Select the Bible book it relates to.
        -   **Answer Options:** Add 2-4 possible answers.
        -   **Correct Answer Index:** `0` for the first option, `1` for the second, etc.
    4.  Click **"Publish"**.

### **Testimonies**

-   **To Add a New Testimony:**
    1.  Click on **"Testimony"** and **"Create new"**.
    2.  Fill in the fields:
        -   **Title:** A catchy title for the testimony.
        -   **Author:** The person’s name.
        -   **Avatar:** Upload a photo of the author.
        -   **Full Testimony:** Write the full story.
        -   **Featured:** Check this to show it on the homepage.
    4.  Click **"Publish"**.
